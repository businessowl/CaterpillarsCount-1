Privacy Policy

1. We do not share your email or other personal information with any other party.

2. One of the Caterpillars Count! mobile app settings allows you to share photos of arthropods you observe on your surveys with iNaturalist, another citizen science website. If you agree to this option, then whenever you submit a survey with an arthropod photo, an observation will be submitted to iNaturalist that includes the date and geographic coordinates of the observation as well as your Caterpillars Count! username.

3. All other information submitted to Caterpillars Count! is completely anonymized.
